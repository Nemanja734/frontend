Explore Tattoo Artists

Browse professional artists in the Showroom, explore their portfolios and choose your favorite one.

Select the Size and Style

Decide what size and style your new tattoo should have in the checkout. This will be the baseline for the time calculation.

Get the price estimation

The algorithm will calculate an estimate for the price based on your input and the hourly rate of the artist.

Schedule a date

Check out the schedule of the artist on the calendar and pick a date of your liking. This is the last step of the checkout.

Go to your appointment

Visit the artist's location on the day of the appointment and get your new tattoo. The artist will log the actual time of the appointment.

Receive a refund

After the artist logged the actual time of the tattoo, you will receive a refund for every full hour difference compared to the estimate!
